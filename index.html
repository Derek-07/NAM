About Us

Nimbus Academy of Management is one of Uttarakhand’s premier management institutes, established in 1998 under the leadership of the Doon Educational Trust Society (DETS)—a group of visionary educationists committed to academic excellence. Guided by our motto, “विद्याविनियोगात समृद्धि” which means “Fostering prosperity through the power of education,” Nimbus is built on the belief that knowledge is the foundation of progress and success.

  • Established in 1998, Nimbus Academy of Management is one of the premier management institutes in Uttarakhand.
  • It is governed by the Doon Educational Trust Society (DETS), a team of experienced and respected educationists.
  • Our motto, “विद्याविनियोगात समृद्धि”, means “Fostering prosperity through the power of education.”
  • Nimbus believes education goes beyond academics—it prepares students to lead in business, innovation, and life.
  • With over 28 years of experience, Nimbus blends academic learning with practical industry exposure.
  • Strong industry tie-ups ensure students gain hands-on understanding of modern business practices.
  • Our teaching approach balances theory with real-world application, making students job-ready and confident.
  • Emphasis is placed on entrepreneurial thinking, leadership development, and a global outlook.
  • Students benefit from workshops, internships, live projects, and industry visits, which enrich their skills.
  • The curriculum is designed to develop problem-solving, communication, and teamwork abilities.
  • Nimbus has earned a strong reputation among top recruiters across sectors, thanks to its skilled graduates.
  • Our consistent placement record reflects the quality of education and student preparedness.
  • Education at Nimbus is about discovering your potential, growing with confidence, and creating a successful future.
