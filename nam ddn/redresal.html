Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to resolve a grievance?
Grievances are typically resolved within 15-30 working days, depending on the complexity and priority level. Urgent matters are addressed within 7 working days.
Can I submit grievances anonymously?
While we encourage providing contact information for follow-up, anonymous grievances can be submitted. However, this may limit our ability to provide updates and gather additional information if needed.
What happens after I submit a grievance?
Upon submission, you'll receive a reference number. Your grievance will be assigned to the appropriate department, and you'll receive regular updates on the progress through email or SMS.
Can I appeal if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?
Yes, you can appeal the decision within 10 working days of receiving the resolution. Appeals are reviewed by a higher authority committee.
