B.C.A

Affiliated to H.N.B. Garhwal University (A Central University), Srinagar, (Uttarakhand)

Join Now
B.C.A

Course Overview

A three-year regular program designed to meet the needs and demands of those aspirants who are looking forward to making their career in IT sector.

ELIGIBILITY

10+2 with Mathematics as a compulsory subject from a recognized Board with a minimum 45% marks.

ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Admission to our BCA program is facilitated either through the CUET scorecard or via merit-based counseling conducted by the college.

CAREER PROSPECTS

In today's technology landscape, Programming Language, Software Development, Data Science, Networking, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence converge to redefine how we engage with technology. IT Support maintains systems, while Machine Learning and AI automate tasks and drive predictive insights. Together, they fuel innovation, transforming industries and societies globally.
