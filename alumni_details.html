Dear Esteemed Alumnus,

Remember those bustling classrooms and the hallowed halls of NIMBUS ACADEMY OF MANAGEMENT (NAM)? It's been over two decades, and we continue to shine brightly as a beacon of knowledge. But our greatest legacy lies not within our walls, but in the triumphs of YOU.

"Grooming Professional with Global Mindset" has been our guiding light, a testament to the unwavering dedication of faculty, staff, administrative personnel, researchers, visiting scholars, industry partners, and most importantly, our exceptional alumni. Our alumni, who have become leaders across a wide range of national and international industries, are the living embodiment of our mission. We are especially grateful to our Alumni Association, whose contributions are woven into the very fabric of our institution.

Today, we're thrilled to unveil our Alumni Platform, a vibrant portal that reconnects you to the beating heart of our academy. This isn't just a virtual space; it's a two-way bridge. We'll share the achievements that make us proud – groundbreaking research, innovative initiatives, and the ever-evolving landscape of our nurturing campus. But more importantly, we want to hear from YOU. Share your stories, your hard-won victories, and the wisdom you've gained. Your journeys inspire the next generation of aspiring minds at NAM.

We invite you back, not just in spirit, but in person. Let NAM's familiar embrace welcome you home. We eagerly anticipate celebrating 25th anniversary with YOU, our most distinguished alumni. Mark your calendars – it's a momentous occasion you won't want to miss.

With warmest regards,
Dr. Rajesh Kumar Tiwari
